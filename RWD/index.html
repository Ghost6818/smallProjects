Health waffles

  • 2 large eggs
  • 65g lupin flour (1/2 cup)
  • 30g green banana flour
  • 30g rice flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 240g unsweetened oat milk
  • 3 tbsp sugar free sweetener

INSTRUCTIONS

Wait for it to heat up, then add one tablespoon of batter to each slot of the waffle iron. In my waffle iron it takes about 4 minutes and you're done! Top them with your favorite fruit and enjoy!

NOTE

If you don't have spirulina you can replace it with your favorite fruits in the blender. If you are lactose intolerant, use lactose free greek yogurt or coconut/soy yogurt. Can use monk fruit or stevia as sweeteners.

French toast

  • 2 slices wholemeal bread
  • 1 egg
  • 100ml milk
  • 1/4 tsp cinamon
  • 5 drops stevia
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp oil of choice

INSTRUCTIONS

In a large/wide bowl, whisk to combine the egg, milk, cinnamon, stevia and vanilla extract. Slice the bread .Heat the oil in a skillet over low heat. Remember Once dipped, immediately transfer to the pan. Fry for 1.5-2 minutes on each side or until golden. Enjoy!

NOTES

Make sure you dip the bread into the egg mixture one slice at a time, otherwise the French toast will become too mushy. Use any toppings you like!

Frozen yoghurt

  • 400g greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp blue spirulina or frozen fruits
  • 1 tbs sweetener of your choice
  • Berries, nuts, rice puffs etc

INSTRUCTIONS

Freeze some greek yogurt in an ice tray. Blend the frozen yogurt cubes with blue spirulina and your chosen sweetener. Top it with berries, nuts, crisps, puffs etc. or any other toppings you like.

NOTES

If you don't have spirulina you can replace it with your favorite fruits in the blender. If you are lactose intolerant, use lactose free greek yogurt or coconut/soy yogurt. Can use monk fruit or stevia as sweeteners.
